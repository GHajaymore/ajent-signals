1. What this policy covers

2. Information we collect

  • Subscription status: trial/paid tier and renewal date, provided to us by Apple App Store or Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store your payment card details — Apple and Google handle billing directly.

  • To monitor performance, fix bugs, and improve signal-engine accuracy and app usability.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Use.
